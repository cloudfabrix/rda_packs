## Overview 
The Base Pack for AI Projects provides a comprehensive collection of artifacts, including dashboards, blueprints, datasets, pipelines, and streams, designed to accelerate the setup and management of AI initiatives. This pack enables teams to quickly deploy essential resources and best practices for AI project administration, monitoring, and analysis.

## Contents

- **Dashboards:** Pre-built dashboards for project administration, conversation analysis, cost observability, workflow tracking, and more.
- **Blueprints:** Templates for model cost observability and other AI project needs.
- **Datasets:** Sample datasets such as LLM model costs to support analytics and reporting.
- **Pipelines:** Automated workflows for cost tracking, user approvals, and workflow execution.
- **Streams:** Data streams for prompt templates, toolsets, and other AI project components.

## Restoring Artifacts in Another Environment

To restore the artifacts in a different environment:

1. **Obtain the Pack:** Ensure you have access to the complete artifact pack, including all subfolders and files.
2. **Import Artifacts:** Use your platform's import or restore functionality to upload the contents of the pack. Refer to the platform documentation for specific instructions.
3. **Verify Dependencies:** Check the `manifest.yaml` and `meta_artifacts.json` files for artifact dependencies and ensure all required components are included.
4. **Configure Settings:** Update any environment-specific settings or credentials as needed after restoration.
